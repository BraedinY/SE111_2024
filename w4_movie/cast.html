Cast of the Movie: White House Down (2013)

Channing Tatum

Channing Tatum leads the White House Downcast as John Cale, a military veteran who served in Afghanistan and now works as a Capitol policeman.

Jamie Foxx

Jamie Foxx plays James Sawyer, President of the United States, who is the main target of an attack on the White House.

Jason Clarke

Jason CLarke plays Emil Stenz, an ex-Delta Force and CIA operative who leads a group of mercenaries in infiltrating and taking over the White House.

Maggie Gyllenhaal

Maggie Gyllenhaal plays Carol Wilkes-Finnerty, Secret Service Presidential Detail Deputy Special Agent-in-Charge.

Joey King

Joey King plays Emily Cale, the daughter of John Cale.
